Core Capabilities

Industrial Engineering Services

From structural design to protective coatings, PRS GROUP delivers high-stakes infrastructure solutions for Tier-1 industrial leaders.

PEB & Structural Steel

Our Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) systems are designed for maximum efficiency and structural integrity. We handle everything from high-rise industrial sheds to complex mezzanine systems.

  • Custom Structural Design & Analysis
  • Precision Fabrication & Erection
  • Seismic Resistant Framing Systems
  • Turnkey Warehouse Solutions

Industrial Surface Protection

We specialize in high-spec protective coatings for environments with extreme thermal and chemical exposure. Trusted by NINL and HINDALCO for blast furnace and refinery equipment protection.

  • Epoxy & Polyurethane Systems
  • Sand Blasting (SA 2.5 Standard)
  • Heat Resistant Coatings (up to 600°C)
  • Anti-Corrosive Marine Grade Systems

Railway Infrastructure

PRS GROUP is an expert in private railway siding maintenance and track development for major industrial hubs. Approved for safety-critical works at TATA Steel BSL.

  • Thermit Welding (SKV/PLA)
  • Track Deep Screening & Packing
  • Point & Crossing Overhauling
  • Automated Level Crossing Systems
